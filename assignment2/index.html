Welcome to Sweet Life!

Where Gelato not onlly in Italy.


History

Italian ice cream has long been a favorite dish of many people. That's why over time, ice cream dishes are constantly prepared in new ways. Gradually, ice cream types became rich and diverse such as fresh ice cream, gelato, popsicles, etc. But the most delicious and attractive is definitely gelato ice cream. In 2023, we started learning gelato recipes from the locals and brought you these delicious ice cream flavors.

Menu

Gelato Chocolate

This chocolate ice cream is irresistibly creamy and made with two forms of chocolate for a deep rich flavor. It’s not overly sweet, and is just as delicious (maybe even more-so) than your favorite ice cream shop! Serve it alongside a slice of favorite chocolate cake, a piece of this giant cookie cake or eat it all by itself!

Vanilla Milk Gelato

This Vanilla Milk Gelato is a perfect treat, with a creamy, dense texture and intense vanilla flavor. Vanilla gelato is similar to vanilla bean ice cream, but with milk instead of heavy cream. If you’ve been wanting to experiment with homemade gelato, now is the time to get started!

Gelato Strawberry

Experience the refreshing flavor of Sweet Strawberry, a fruity and delicious ice cream that bursts with the taste of fresh strawberries. With a smooth and creamy texture, this ice cream is made with high-quality ingredients for an irresistible summer treat.
